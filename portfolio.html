Scratch Game

In this game, the goal is to get through the mazes in order to help the hedgehog find his burrow.

If you touch the black walls you are then sent back to the beginning of the maze. There are also grasshoppers

throughout the maze which you can collect in order to gain points.

MIT App Inventor Project

This is a GPA calculator. You record the grades you recieved for a certain semester and your overall GPA for the semester is calculated.

There is also an option to state whether or not the class was an AP class which in turn affects your GPA. Once your GPA is calculated,

the GPA appears on the screen along with a photo that corresponds with the GPA.

NetLogo Simulation Project

For my simulation I modified an already existing firework simulation. Thanks to corona virus we probably won't be getting a 4th of July.

However, now you can experience 4th of July fireworks from the comfort of your own home since I have modified the fireworks to strictly be

red, white and blue. In order to do this, I went into the code and removed all base colors that were not red, white or blue.
